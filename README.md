🎮 Call of Duty Monopoly (Web App)

A browser-based multiplayer board game inspired by the classic Monopoly experience, reimagined with a Call of Duty theme. Play directly in your browser—no downloads required.

🔗 Live Demo: https://call-of-duty-monopoly.web.app/

📌 Overview

Call of Duty Monopoly is a web-based game that blends traditional Monopoly mechanics with elements inspired by the Call of Duty universe. Players compete to dominate the board by acquiring properties, collecting rent, and strategically outplaying opponents.

Like classic Monopoly-style games, the goal is to build wealth and bankrupt other players through smart decisions and resource management.
